Why Choose Dore Canine Services?

At Dore Canine Services, walks are always about quality, not quantity. I never take out more than 4 dogs at a time, so every one gets the attention, care, and time they deserve. After a muddy adventure, they'll be towelled down before heading home, and as a Dore resident I understand the responsibility of looking after our community as well as our dogs. 

And yes, I always pick up the poo!
